Our Story

Where every child is known, nurtured, and challenged to grow

Kinder Bubble International School (KBIS) on Spintex Road welcomes families with children from six months through ten years. We blend structured routines with joyful discovery, guided by experienced educators — including Principal Ms. Delphina Naa Lomoley Lomotey and a team vetted for safety and care.

First impressions

Children step into classrooms and shared areas designed for curiosity — where play and structure work together, just as families experience on our Spintex campus.

Every age, known by name

From creche through primary, small groups and caring adults help each child build confidence, language, and friendships that carry into the next stage.

Cambridge roots, bilingual rhythm

Our Cambridge-aligned pathway blends literacy, numeracy, science, and the arts with English and French exposure — so learners grow as thoughtful global citizens.

Community that extends beyond the gate

Open communication with families, rolling admissions, and a full day on campus (6 a.m.–6 p.m.) keep KBIS woven into the rhythm of life in Accra.

Our mission

To nurture confident, kind, and capable learners through a blend of Cambridge-aligned academics, bilingual exposure, and joyful discovery — meeting each family where they are.

Our vision

A vibrant Accra campus where children from six months to ten years feel safe to explore, known by name, and prepared for the wider world.

Core Values

Academic excellence

Cambridge-inspired learning with language arts, math, science, and handwriting woven into engaging daily rhythms.

Community & care

Teachers partner closely with families, prioritising wellbeing, communication, and a welcoming culture on Spintex Road.

Joyful discovery

Hands-on projects, music, and movement help children take risks in learning while still feeling emotionally secure.

Global perspective

English and French exposure, Cambridge-aligned planning, and respect for Ghanaian context prepare learners to think broadly.
